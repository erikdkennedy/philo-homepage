Infrastructure Benefits

Go IPTV

Students bring their own devices; TVs can be anywhere there is internet, powered by Roku streaming players. Maintaining your current cable system in parallel to IPTV is an option.

Cost Avoidance

Put your TVs anywhere– no need to wire cable as long as their is connectivity. Don’t need coaxial cable installed in new residence halls. Discontinue maintenance costs of coaxial cable system.

Built for Campus Networks

Adaptive bitrate streaming. Traffic doesn’t leave your LAN, so external bandwidth isn’t strained.
